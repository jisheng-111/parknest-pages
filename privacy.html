Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 14, 2026

ParkNest respects your privacy. This app is designed to store your parking information locally on your device.

Information We Use

ParkNest may use the following information only to provide app functionality:

  • Location: to save where you parked and help guide you back later
  • Photos: if you choose to add parking reference photos
  • Notifications: if you choose to use parking timer reminders

How Data Is Stored

Your parking spot data, notes, labels, timer information, and optional photos are stored locally on your device.

Data Sharing

ParkNest does not sell your personal data. The app does not require an account and does not use cloud storage for your parking data.

Third-Party Services

If you choose to open your saved location in a map app, that action may transfer location information to the selected map provider.

Your Choices

You can control location, photo, and notification permissions at any time in your device settings.
